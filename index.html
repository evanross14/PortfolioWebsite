ABOUT ME

Academics

As a Computer Science and Engineering student at Michigan State University (Go Green!), I have immersed myself in a curriculum that has sharpened my technical skills and problem-solving abilities. Over the years, I have completed coursework in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, and C++, gaining a strong foundation in programming and software development. These languages have equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of coding structures, making it easier to adapt to less familiar or older programming languages. My academic journey has been driven by a passion for innovation and a desire to apply technology to solve real-world problems.

Recent Work Experience

During my time at the Kellogg Hotel and Conference Center, I gained valuable professional experience in two distinct roles. Initially, as a front desk associate, I developed excellent communication and interpersonal skills by assisting guests, handling check-ins, and addressing their needs. This role taught me the importance of professionalism and adaptability in customer-facing environments.

Later, I transitioned to the Audio Visual Department, where I delved into more technical work more closely related to my field of study. I learned how to manage sound levels on mixers, differentiate between Line and Mic outputs, and set up projectors, screens, and other AV equipment. This role also required effective communication, as I often was confronted with situations where I needed to explain technical processes to clients in an accessible manner. These experiences bridged the gap between technical expertise and client interaction, enhancing my ability to collaborate and problem-solve.

Hobbies

Beyond academics and work, my hobbies reflect my passion for technology and personal growth. hosting websites, and many other projects which allow me to explore the practical applications of my technical knowledge. Creating simple programs and games is a pastime that keeps my skills sharp and fosters my creativity.

Running is another significant part of my life. It offers a chance to stay physically healthy while also giving me time to reflect and organize my thoughts. This balance between physical activity and intellectual curiosity fuels my overall growth and well-being. Without running and working out, the things I enjoy would not feel so full. Part of what keeps me happy, sane and confident is working out.

Outcome

The combination of my academic pursuits, professional experience, and hobbies has shaped me into a well-rounded individual with a unique blend of technical expertise and interpersonal skills. My coursework has provided a strong foundation in computer science, while my time at the Kellogg Hotel and Conference Center has honed my communication and adaptability.

Engaging in hobbies like building home servers and robotics has deepened my technical understanding, while running has taught me discipline and the value of mental clarity. These experiences have not only made me a more capable problem-solver but also a more empathetic and effective collaborator. As I move forward, I am confident in my ability to navigate challenges and contribute meaningfully to any team or project.
